GAGNER SANS DOMINATE NI PRESENCE (Montreal version)

This is the winning deck of Hugues Bertrand for the tournament Theo Bell
Invitational, held in Montreal on September 8, 2001.  It's a new version of the deck he
played at the Lure of the Serpent tournament.  Note: The name of the deck can be
translated something like this:  "Winning without Dominate and Presence"
