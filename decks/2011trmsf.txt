A Twist of Madness

The classic Henry Taylor-twister deck where Henry ends up
blocking everything and ousting with Smiling Jack. I felt the deck was
a bit too passive, so added Clan Impersonation and Madness Network
which allows for some serious end game bleed punch with Anima
Gathering and Earth Control stealth. Bleeding at the end of your
predator's turn gives you the edge and a pool which pays for Jack. 31
cards is too much, but there's some nifty mechanics that adds
flexibility to the classic deck.
