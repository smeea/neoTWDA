Demented Midgets

'Inspired' by a deck played by Erol Hammer at the German
Championship 2007. Nothing new or fancy. 'Wrong and Crosswise' was
huge, as was 'Kaymakli Fragment + Midget'. If your sitting next to a
Rush-Combat Deck, the game is over. Payload should be slightly
increased.
