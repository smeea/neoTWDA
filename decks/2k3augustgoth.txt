The Rise and Fall of Martin Franckel

This is a fun deck. And good.
Get Pier 13 and set some stuff up, wait for a while and then you win.
WIthout Laptop, no VP's (move the Laptops with Heidelberg when you're
ousting someone).
Heidelberg kicks ass. Martin Franckel should be the last vampire you
influence up. He is best used with Arcane Library.
